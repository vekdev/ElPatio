Welcome to El Patio

We are a family-run restaurant located in Muro on the island of Mallorca. We are passionate about our meat - and you can taste it with every bite. Come and relax in our patio and watch our chef cook your meat exactly the way you like it on our wood burning grill. We can't wait to welcome you.

Only the best will do

The first step to great tasting meat is the quality of that meat! Every day we select only the best and freshest meat available, and you can taste the difference with every single bite. Whether it's our succulent fillet steak or our delicious veal & pork kebab that takes your fancy, you can be sure that it will taste amazing and make you want to come back again and again.

Find us

You can find us in the tourist area of Playa de Muro, in the El Patio center near the Alcudia Suite hotel, and just 150m from the beach. If you are arriving by car, you will normally find plenty of parking along Avinguda de s'Albufera.
